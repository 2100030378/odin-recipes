Pizza

Description

Pizza is a universally loved dish that combines a crispy crust, flavorful tomato sauce, melted cheese, and a variety of toppings. This recipe is perfect for a homemade treat that's both delicious and customizable to suit any taste.

This homemade pizza features a golden crust, rich tomato sauce, and gooey cheese, topped with fresh and savory ingredients. It's a crowd-pleaser that brings everyone to the table for a slice of happiness.

Ingredients

  • 1 premade pizza dough or homemade dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni (optional)
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness and transfer it to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  3. Brush the dough with olive oil and evenly spread the tomato sauce over it.
  4. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the sauce, followed by Parmesan cheese.
  5. Add your toppings such as pepperoni, bell peppers, mushrooms, and any other desired ingredients.
  6. Sprinkle with dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  7. Bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes.
  9. Garnish with fresh basil leaves, slice, and serve immediately. Enjoy your homemade pizza!
