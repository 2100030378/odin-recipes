Nachos

Description

Nachos are a vibrant and versatile dish that layers crispy tortilla chips with melted cheese, savory toppings, and zesty flavors. This recipe combines fresh, flavorful ingredients to create a crowd-pleasing snack that's perfect for parties, movie nights, or casual get-togethers.

These homemade nachos are piled high with crispy tortilla chips, gooey melted cheese, and a medley of savory toppings. They’re a crowd-pleasing snack that's perfect for sharing and bursting with bold flavors in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 large bag of tortilla chips
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup cooked ground beef or shredded chicken (optional)
  • 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup jalapeño slices (optional)
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup guacamole
  • 1/4 cup salsa

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Spread a layer of tortilla chips evenly on a large baking sheet or oven-safe platter.
  3. Sprinkle half of the shredded cheddar cheese over the chips.
  4. Top with cooked ground beef or shredded chicken, black beans, diced tomatoes, black olives, and jalapeño slices (if using).
  5. Sprinkle the remaining cheese evenly over the toppings.
  6. Bake in the oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly.
  8. Top with fresh cilantro, dollops of sour cream, guacamole, and salsa.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy your nachos!
