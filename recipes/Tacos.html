Tacos

Description

Tacos are a vibrant and versatile dish that wraps a variety of flavorful fillings in a soft or crispy tortilla shell. This recipe brings together fresh, bold ingredients to create a quick and satisfying meal for any occasion.

These homemade tacos feature seasoned meat or plant-based alternatives, fresh veggies, and zesty toppings, all packed into a tortilla for a burst of flavor in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 8 small tortillas (soft or hard-shell)
  • 1 pound ground beef, chicken, or plant-based protein
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup guacamole
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat and add the olive oil.
  2. Add the ground beef (or alternative protein) and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Stir in the taco seasoning and a small amount of water (as directed on the seasoning packet). Simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave for a few seconds until pliable.
  5. Assemble the tacos by adding the seasoned meat to the center of each tortilla.
  6. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, onions, cheese, and any other desired toppings.
  7. Add dollops of sour cream, guacamole, and salsa for extra flavor.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy your tacos!
